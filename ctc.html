About Crack The Code

We are excited to present "Crack The Code," a two-phase coding and hackathon event hosted by Bandipur House and designed to challenge and inspire students at a pan-BS level. The event will take place on 27th and 29th December, beginning with an elimination round where participants will compete in a rigorous coding contest, testing their problem-solving skills and algorithmic knowledge.

  • Phase 1: Elimination Round: Coding Contest to assess problem-solving skills and algorithmic knowledge.
  • Phase 2: Final Round (Hackathon): A real-world project-based problem-solving challenge to encourage creativity and technical expertise.
  • When: 27th & 29th December
  • Who Can Participate: Open to all students at the pan-BS level.
  • Host: Bandipur House

The top performers will advance to the final round, where they will tackle real-world project-based problems, applying their technical expertise to create innovative solutions under time constraints. Hosted by Bandipur House, this event fosters creativity, collaboration, and a taste of real-world project development and presentation.

Evaluation Criteria

The evaluation of participants will be conducted using an automated, certified judging and analytics system integrated into the event platform. This system ensures consistent and objective evaluation, focusing on key criteria that are essential for success in both rounds of the competition.

Problem-Solving Skills

Key Focus: Accuracy, efficiency, and optimization of solutions.

Innovation

Key Focus: Creativity and originality, especially in the Hackathon round.

Code Quality

Key Focus: Readability, structure, and maintainability of code.

Collaboration & Presentation

Key Focus: Ability to communicate and present solutions, especially in the final round.
